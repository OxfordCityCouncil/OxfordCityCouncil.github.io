Understand User Needs

  • Start with what customers want to do, not what we want them to do

  • Use data, not assumptions, about what their needs are

Iterate and Improve

  • Fail fast and learn - don't expect perfection

  • Keep listening to customer feedback to guide improvements

Make forms easy to use

  • Know why you're asking every question, and only ask what is needed

  • Do the hard work to make it easy for users
