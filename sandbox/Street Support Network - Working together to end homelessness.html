Street Support Network in Portsmouth

Find Help Give Help

Street Support Network connects people experiencing homelessness, charities, voluntary groups, businesses and kind-hearted folk to work together to end homelessness. You can find support services for people experiencing homelessness, and DO something to help - offering your time, money or resources to local organisations.

Supporting homeless people in Portsmouth

We share the collective responsibility to reduce rough sleeping in Portsmouth through a consistent approach of good working practices, coordination, and communication; to assist those wanting to make positive moves off the street into safe and sustainable accommodation.

If you are a Portsmouth based organisation, please register your details, or get in touch.

If you are a local business and able to help, support and develop this resource, please get in touch to find out how you could help.

Street Support Network is currently available in Greater Manchester, Leeds and Bournemouth, and we would love to share these tools with more locations. If you think Street Support might be useful in your area, please get in touch.

We are looking for businesses and organisations to partner with us so that we can continue to develop this resource.

If you share our vision of a world without homelessness, please get in touch.
